Your interior painter in Amsterdam

Executing good interior painting is a special skill. And we love that profession. Decorating, restoring, and maintaining special and old homes, offices and buildings in Amsterdam – that is our specialty. And it has been for years. Both the paintwork inside as well as outside. From beautiful old mansions, canal houses and 1930s homes to the houses on the ‘new’ islands. By immersing ourselves in the details of each case that differs per client, we deliver quality as to be expected. Quality that you naturally expect from us. You want the very best – we want to deliver it. Sometimes with a standard solution. More often with tailor-made solutions. But always in a carefree manner.

We choose quality

Sustainable quality is very important to us. With an emphasis on sustainability. Wijnands Schilderwerken strives to use the best materials with the lowest possible environmental impact. In order to guarantee our quality, we are partners of De Betere Schilder and we are Sikkens Quality Painter. This of course also applies to our interior painting. The paint products are carefully selected for each situation. Our qualified professionals only use solution- and odorless paints indoor, so that the inconvenience can be minimized. And before opening the first paint can, the workplace is professionally taped for optimum protection of your property. Everything so that we can guarantee perfect craftmanship.

Color advice

The indoor painting in your home can strongly determine the atmosphere of your interior. Our painters know what color can do to a room. Do you want a spacious and airy room? Or rather cosy and homely? The right combinations of colors and effects will make the room spark. Exactly the way you want it. We gladly advise you on this. From the use of color, to using special paints, such as a ‘concrete look’ in collaboration with brsh, or washable paints.
